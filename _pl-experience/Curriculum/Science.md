<p>The goals of Science education are:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Fostering Scientific Literacy:</strong> To nurture students to
make informed decisions and take responsible actions in their daily lives.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Building Strong Science Fundamentals: </strong>To equip students
to innovate and pursue STEM for future learning and careers.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Science.png">
</div>
<p></p>
<p>We aim to nurture <strong>PL-Lites</strong> who are:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>INspired by Science</strong>: Cultivating a passion for learning,
appreciating its relevance, and seeing its potential to address global
challenges.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Able to INquire like Scientists</strong>: Developing strong scientific
fundamentals and engaging confidently in inquiry and critical thinking.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Ready to INnovate using Science</strong>: Applying knowledge creatively
to solve real-world problems and contribute meaningfully to society.</p>
</li>
</ul>
