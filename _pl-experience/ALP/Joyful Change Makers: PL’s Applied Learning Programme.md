<p>Our school's Applied Learning Programme, "Joyful Change-Makers", integrates
STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics) with Maker
Education and Design Thinking to empower students as creative problem-solvers
and innovators.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Picture6.png">

<p><strong><u>Key Outcomes and Approaches:</u></strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Interdisciplinary Approach:</strong>&nbsp;Combines STEAM subjects
to foster creativity and innovation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Hands-on Learning:</strong>&nbsp;Maker Education provides exploratory,
experiential activities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Design Thinking:</strong>&nbsp;Students learn systematic problem-solving
through the Design Thinking-ABC methodology</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>21st Century Competencies:</strong>&nbsp;Develops critical skills
like communication, collaboration, and computational thinking through our
C-CUBE synthesis emphasis.</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p><strong><u>Programme:</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Our ALP is built on three pillars:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Joyful Discovery:</strong>&nbsp;Exploratory maker education activities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Joyful Growth:</strong>&nbsp;Iterative design thinking process</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Joyful Service:</strong>&nbsp;Real-world STEAM applications with

<p><strong>Learning Outcomes:</strong>
</p>

</li>
<li>
<p>Communication and collaboration skills</p>
</li>
<li>

</li>
<li>
<p>A passion for lifelong learning and service</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Through engaging projects and partnerships, our Joyful Change-Makers programme
prepares students to shape a better future as Creators, Connectors and
Contributors.</p>
