| Level | Recess |
|---|---|
| P1 | 8.35am – 9.05am* |
| P2 | 9.05am – 9.35am* |
| P3 | 9.35am – 10.05am* |
| P4 | 10.05am – 10.35am* |

| P6 | 11.05am – 11.35am** |

**Note**&nbsp;:  
*P1 to P4 will have their 5 minutes Snack Break between 11:35am to 12:05pm following their respective class timetable.
